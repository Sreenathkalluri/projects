Financial Terms Explained

Open Price

The price at which a stock first trades when the market opens for the day.

High Price

The highest price at which a stock traded during the trading day.

Low Price

The lowest price at which a stock traded during the trading day.

Close Price

The final price at which a stock is traded when the market closes.

Volume

The total number of shares traded during a specific time period.

VWAP

Volume Weighted Average Price - the average price weighted by trading volume.
